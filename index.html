Nighttime Routine

How I relax and unwind

Play a Game

I begin my routine by playing a few games on my Switch. This helps me to destress and relax. I often play Balatro, a card game.

Watch Television

While playing a game, I often watch television with my family. My mom and I like to watch Gilmore Girls or the news together.

Dinnertime

Around 7pm, I eat dinner with my family.

Getting Ready for Bed

I take pride in my skincare routine, it is a part of my day I enjoy and look forward to. Skincare is a form of self-care for me and helps make me feel confident in my own skin.

Catching Up

After I do my skincare routine, I love to catch up with my friends by chatting with them on Discord. I often play games with my friends while we talk, which makes the conversations even more fun and engaging.

Dozing Off

At the end of the night, I reflect on the day's events and prepare to sleep. I often journal my thoughts and feelings for the day before deciding to rest my mind for the day.
